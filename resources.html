About EarthWatch

EarthWatch is a web platform focused on earthquake and disaster information. We provide users with simple earthquake and weather data through open APIs such as USGS (United States Geological Survey) and OpenWeatherMap.

We offer:

  • Global earthquake maps and information
  • Disaster prevention reminders
  • YouTube live earthquake monitoring
